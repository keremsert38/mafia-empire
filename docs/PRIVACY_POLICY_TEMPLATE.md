# Privacy Policy - Mafia Empire

**Last Updated: [BUGÜNÜN TARİHİ]**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mafia Empire ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Username
- Player statistics and game progress
- In-app purchase history

### Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- IP address
- App version

### Game Data
- Game progress and achievements
- In-game currency (MT Coins)
- Business and territory ownership
- Family membership
- Leaderboard rankings

### Usage Information
- App usage statistics
- Feature interactions
- Session duration
- Crash reports and diagnostics

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the game service
- Process in-app purchases
- Sync game progress across devices
- Improve game features and user experience
- Analyze app performance and fix bugs
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Send important updates and notifications

## Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure
- Payment processing is handled by Apple App Store/Google Play
- We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
- Access to personal data is restricted and monitored

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Supabase
- Purpose: Database and authentication
- Data Collected: User account, game progress
- Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

### RevenueCat
- Purpose: In-app purchase management
- Data Collected: Purchase history, device identifiers
- Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Apple App Store / Google Play
- Purpose: Payment processing
- Data Collected: Payment information, purchase history
- Privacy Policy: 
  - Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  - Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## In-App Purchases

- All purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play
- We do not store your payment information
- Purchase history is retained for customer support
- Refunds are subject to Apple/Google policies

## Children's Privacy

Mafia Empire is rated 17+ and is not intended for children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Export your game data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@mafiaempire.app

## Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Game progress: Retained for 90 days after account deletion
- Purchase history: Retained for 7 years for legal compliance
- Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized after 180 days

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an in-app notification

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us:

**Email:** support@mafiaempire.app  
**Website:** https://mafiaempire.app/support
